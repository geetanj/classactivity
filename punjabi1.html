Dance in India comprises numerous styles of dances, generally classified as classical or folk.

CLASICAL DANCE

Classical dance in India has developed a type of dance-drama that is a form of a total theater. There are main eight Indian classical dance style.But we are going to discuss only 3 of them.

Bharatanatyam (Tamil Nadu):-Bharatanatyam is a classical dance from the South Indian state of Tamil Nadu. Bharatanatyam is a major genre of Indian classical dance that originated in the Hindu temples of Tamil Nadu and neighboring regions.

Kathakali:-In this katha means “story” nad kali means “performance”.It is a highly stylized classical dance-drama form which originated from Kerala in the 17th century.

KATHAK:-Kathak is traditionally attributed to the traveling bards of ancient northern India, known as Kathakars or storytellers.

FOLK DANCE

Folk dances and plays in India retain significance in rural areas as the expression of the daily work and rituals of village communities. we can see these dance in videos also like.
